Classic Apple Crisp

A cozy autumn classic with cinnamon-spiced apples and a buttery oat topping. Serve warm with vanilla ice cream!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups peeled, sliced apples
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 6 tbsp cold butter

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Grease a baking dish.
  2. Toss apples with sugar and cinnamon. Spread in the dish.
  3. Mix oats, flour, brown sugar, and butter until crumbly.
  4. Sprinkle topping over apples.
  5. Bake 40-45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
