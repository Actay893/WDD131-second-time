Content

Home page

Our Mission Vesta is dedicated to helping refugee individuals and families rebuild their lives with dignity, stability, and a sense of home. Through essential resources, community connections, and compassionate support, we work to create welcoming environments where new beginnings are possible.

Our Story Founded by Brooke and Summer Williamson, Vesta began after a life-changing experience during the holiday season. The founders' parents signed up to “adopt” a refugee family for the holidays. Hearing their story and everything they had endured left a deep impression. It felt heartbreaking to see the hardships they had faced, yet incredibly inspiring to witness their strength and determination to build a new life in an unfamiliar place, far from their home, friends, family, and culture. Knowing they were just one of many families forced to flee and face unimaginable struggles became the driving force behind Vesta’s creation.

What We Do Vesta supports refugee families by: • Providing essential household and personal items • Connecting families with local resources and community support • Creating welcoming environments that help refugees feel valued and safe • Supporting individuals as they rebuild their lives from the ground up

Our Values Our work is guided by compassion, dignity, and respect. We believe every person deserves to feel safe, welcomed, and supported, regardless of where they come from or what they have endured. Vesta is built on the belief that small acts of kindness can create lasting change.

Our Vision Vesta strives to create communities where every refugee family is met with warmth, opportunity, and a true sense of belonging. By helping individuals rebuild their lives, we aim to turn hardship into hope and transition into opportunity.
