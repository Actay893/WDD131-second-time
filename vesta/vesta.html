🚀

Our Mission

Vesta is dedicated to helping refugee individuals and families rebuild their lives with dignity, stability, and a sense of home. Through essential resources, community connections, and compassionate support, we work to create welcoming environments where new beginnings are possible.

✨

Our Story

Founded by Brooke and Summer Williamson, Vesta began after a life-changing experience during the holiday season. The founders' parents signed up to “adopt” a refugee family for the holidays. Hearing their story and everything they had endured left a deep impression. It felt heartbreaking to see the hardships they had faced, yet incredibly inspiring to witness their strength and determination to build a new life in an unfamiliar place, far from their home, friends, family, and culture. Knowing they were just one of many families forced to flee and face unimaginable struggles became the driving force behind Vesta’s creation.

📡

What We Do

Vesta supports refugee families by:

• Providing essential household and personal items

• Connecting families with local resources and community support

• Creating welcoming environments that help refugees feel valued and safe

• Supporting individuals as they rebuild their lives from the ground up
